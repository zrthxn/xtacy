Rules and Regulations:
-Number of players per team – 5 (on field) + 1(substitute).
-Knockout based tournament.
-Teams are advised to wear colour coordinated jerseys.
-In case of any misconduct, the match refree has the authority to disqualify or take any action against a player or the team.
-In case of a tie, 2 extra minutes will be rewarded followed by a penalty shoutout and then sudden death.
-Rolling substitutes.
-Participants will be allowed to play in proper kit only.
-Referee’s decision will be final and binding.
-Registration fees will not be refunded once paid.
-Certificates and medals to be awarded to the winners.

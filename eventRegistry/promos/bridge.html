"We build too many walls and not enough bridges." -Isaac Newton

So lace up your shoes-
Xtacy'19, the annual fest of the department of engineering and technology, jamia millia islmia, gives you the opportunity to bridge the gap and show your potential in Bridge Building Arena
Participating students get to experience what it is to be an engineer, designing structures to a set of specifications and then see them perform their function!

Rules:
A team must have 2 members.
Teams are required to report an hour prior to the event.
Teams are to construct bridges using only popsicle sticks, glue, tooth picks, and dental floss and other recycled products.

Bridges are to be judged on aesthetics and originality of design, presentation, ultimate load carrying capacity, strength-to-weight ratio, and predicted failure point.

Join us on: 31st MARCH 2019
Venue: FET Building
Time: 10 a.m.

Prizes:

2nd Prize: Rs. 500/-

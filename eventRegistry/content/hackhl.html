HACKATHON

“Make it work, make it right, make it fast.”

Xtacy the annual fest of Faculty of Engineering and Technology, Jamia Millia Islamia, brings to you an offline hackathon -
HacX, where your crazy ideas become reality.

Six hours, ten teams, one room, one problem.

Teams will be given a problem statement on the spot and are expected to develop a solution . The criteria of judgement would be :-
- Approach to the problem
- Practicality in teams of real world application
- Cost effectiveness of the solution
- Reach of the solution to the targeted audience

Venue :- FTK, Jamia Millia Islamia, Delhi
Date :- 31th March, 2019
Time :- 11am

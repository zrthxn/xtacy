Line Following Robot

Can you build an robot capable of following a line that can make key decisions about its path? Then this challenge if for you.

Xtacy, Anual Techno-Cultural Fest of Faculty of Engineering and Technology, Jamia Millia Islamia brings to you

LINE FOLLOWER ROBOT CHALLENGE

The challenge of the competition is to make a robot that can move on black lines on a white background and reach the finish as soon as possible.
Accept the challenge and show your robotic skills.


Team Size: Upto 4 members

Venue : Faculty Of Engineering And Technology

Date : 31st March

Time : 12 PM onwards

Rules :-
1. A Team may consist of Maximum of 4 Members.
2. The maximum overall dimensions of the bot should be 25cm x 25cm x 15cm (l x b x h).
3. You can use any micro controller to program your robot. Pre-programmed kits such as LEGO are Not allowed.
4. The Robot must be powered by a power source such as a Battery fixed on the Robot.
5. The line will be Black and 2 cm in width .
6. There could be 90 degree Turns, acute angle turns, Obtuse angle turns, and crossover .
7. The robot must be controlled autonomously without any human aid after start of the run.
8. If the bot goes out of the track there will be reset with penelty.
9. Judge's Decision will be Final.


Registration Fees - 100 (per team)

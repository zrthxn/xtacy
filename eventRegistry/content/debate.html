Rules and Regulations
-Every candidate will be given 3 mins for the pre-prepared speech and 3 mins for query adressal.
-Each speaker is questioned as soon as they conclude their speech
-He who asserts must prove. In order to establish an assertion the candidate must be prepared with logic, evidence and wit.
-The questioners may ask fair and unambiguous questions that have direct relation with the topic at hand.

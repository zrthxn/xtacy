XTACY 2019
The Writer's Place

To write means more than putting pretty words on a page; The act of writing is to share a part of your soul with the world.

Xtacy, the annual cultural and technical fest of Faculty of Engineering and Technology, Jamia Millia Islamia calls you to test your writing skills in a fun creative writing contest: The Writer's Place.

Date:30th March 2019
Venue:FET Department
Time:12 pm
Registration fee: 20rs per person
Prize Money:
1st place: 300rs
2nd pace: 200rs

Words- so innocent and powerless as they are, as standing in a dictionary, how potent for good and evil they become in the hands of one who knows how to combine them.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:
Participants will be given to 12 keywords. You can choose any 5 and use them in your content. The theme of your piece should be reflected in how you use these words You can write anything: poem, essay, story, article, etc according to your choice.There will be a time limit and the best pieces will be selected for 1st and 2nd position.

On-spot registrations are also allowed.
